Product Design

Many people around the world have built Precious Plastic machines but are still struggling with the design of object. We will help this process with a series of tutorials and guides on how to get the most from our machines.

Digital Network

We need to make it easier for people to connect & collaborate. We want to improve our network to enable fast connection between people, businesses and resources making it easier to get started.

Starter Kits

We will facilitate global adoption by providing starter kits that will make it easier for people to find the most difficult parts to build the machines. We will establish partnerships with companies and provide kits for each machine.

Start a business

We want to create an open source business plan on how to start your own plastic recycling business. Working with entrepreneurs and business experts we want to push bottom up social innovation across the planet.
