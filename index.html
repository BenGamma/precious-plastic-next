Money & People Campaign

We needed a way to make it possible for everyone to help out, no matter what background or wallet size. We created a Money & People Campaign to do this.
 
You can help with what you can, code in your spare time from your laptop in India or donate your birthday cash from the US. After all, plastic waste is a problem affecting us all. 

Money and People are core elements of any project, greatly interconnected and depend on one other. The more people helping, the less funding needed, and vice versa. Metrics on the website are update daily based on your awesomeness.
