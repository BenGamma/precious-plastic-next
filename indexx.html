Precious Plastic. Version 3.0
Better. Together.

Better, how?

Product Design

Many people around the world have built Precious Plastic machines but are still struggling with the design of object. We will help this process with a series of tutorials and guides on how to get the most from our machines.

Digital Network

We need to make it easier for people to connect & collaborate. We want to develop a network to enable easy connection between people, businesses and resources to make it easier to get started.

Starter Kits

We want to make it easier for people to source the most difficult parts to build the machines. We will establish partnerships with companies and provide kits for each machine.

Money

We want to create guides on how to start your own plastic recycling business. Working with experienced entrepreneurs and business experts we want to facilitate bottom up social innovation across the planet.
